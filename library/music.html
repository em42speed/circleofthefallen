Music

Music is an incredibly important aspect in almost any religion. It connects people, enhances religious rites, and brings us closer to our gods. But Satanism struggles with the unique challenge of having a considerable swath of musicians who make use of our aesthetics and themes without actually participating in our religion. We at Circle of the Fallen hold no ill will towards such artists, but we do feel it is important to help each other find music made by actual Satanists.

Here is our collection.

General

  • Bands That Are In On It by Caine Del Sol
    • This spreadsheet is a labor of love by a Satanic author and member of the Satanic band, Grave Gnosis. Caine Del Sol created this in order to track down and verify as many Satanic and occult bands as possible. And when we say Satanic and occult, we’re not playing around with costumes or groups who use dark imagery just to be edgy, we’re talking about actual practitioners who also make music. The sheet is in alphabetical order, includes notes on genre and proof, and even has a suggestions tab where readers can suggest bands for Caine to investigate and possibly add to the list.
